This application was created by Thomas Park.

This application is best experienced with a Windows PC and Google Chrome or the Internet Explorer browser(s). Other setups might not function.

Mixing Tips:
Press play controls if media doesn't autostart.
All media loops until a random amount of time has passed.
At that point a new set of sounds is loaded.
Change the mix over time.
Use mutes to isolate sounds.
Play tracks at different times.
